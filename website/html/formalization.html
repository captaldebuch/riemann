Workstreams

What the repository is organising

Each workstream links to inspectable records rather than using progress percentages as a substitute for verification.

Classical identities

H13 — cotangent and period-function work

Definitions and Lean declarations around cotangent sums, period functions, and the Nyman–Beurling setting. Inspect the associated modules and any explicitly recorded assumptions in the registry.

Quantitative analysis

H14 — zeta and Möbius bounds

Formal scaffolding for zero-free regions, Mellin/Perron techniques, and quantitative Möbius estimates. Classical analytic inputs remain distinguishable from checked Lean declarations.

Active research

H15 — BCF cancellation

The logarithmic-taper BCF direction is recorded as research. Its unresolved cancellation statements are labelled as open gates, not presented as established results.

Functional route

Nyman–Beurling bridge

The functional-analytic equivalence route connects historic work by Nyman, Beurling, and Báez-Duarte to the project’s formal interface.

How to read the status labels

“Proved” means the indexed Lean declaration contains no recorded sorry in its source block; “assumed” and “contains sorry” are explicit flags. These are repository facts, not independent mathematical certification.
