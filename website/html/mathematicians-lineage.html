Original analytic question

Bernhard Riemann

Riemann’s 1859 paper on the prime-counting function introduced the zeta-function setting from which the hypothesis arose.

Classical analysis

Jacques Hadamard and Charles-Jean de la Vallée Poussin

The 1896 prime-number-theorem work and zero-free-region methods are the historic background for the project’s quantitative H14 route.

Functional equivalence

Bertil Nyman, Arne Beurling, and Luis Báez-Duarte

Nyman’s Hilbert-space formulation, Beurling’s closure problem, and Báez-Duarte’s strengthened criterion form the Nyman–Beurling route.

Cotangent and reciprocity work

Sandro Bettin, Brian Conrey, Juan S. Auli, Abdelmejid Bayad, and Matthias Beck

Their papers provide named, citable context for period functions, cotangent sums, and the active H15 research direction.
