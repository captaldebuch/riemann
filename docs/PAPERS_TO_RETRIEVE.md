## Success Criteria

A retrieved paper counts as containing the bound if it proves:

$$\exists C : \forall N, A \in [1,N], \quad \left|\sum_{k=1}^{N} \mu(k) \cdot f(A/k)\right| \le \frac{C}{\log^2(N+2)}$$

where $f$ is either:
- The sawtooth $B_1(x) = x - \lfloor x \rfloor - 1/2$, OR
- A transformation to an equivalent form with a known bound

**Parameters must match**:
- ✓ Uniform in $A$
- ✓ O(1/log²N) rate (or demonstrably better)
- ✓ Under Mertens decay hypothesis or weaker
- ✗ Conditional on RH or intermediate open problem
